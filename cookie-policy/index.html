What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can control them.

Cookies We Use

This website uses the following categories of cookies:

  • ›Strictly Necessary Cookies: Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled. No personal data is collected.
  • ›Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors use the site (pages visited, time on site). Data is aggregated and anonymised. We use privacy-friendly analytics that do not track individuals across sites.
  • ›Preference Cookies: Remember settings such as language preference. Used to improve your experience on return visits.

Third-Party Cookies

If affiliate links are present on this site, clicking them may result in third-party cookies being set by the linked operator's website. We do not control these cookies. Please refer to the relevant operator's cookie policy for details.

How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Instructions vary by browser — consult your browser's help documentation for guidance. Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may affect website functionality. You can also opt out of analytics tracking by enabling Do Not Track in your browser settings.
