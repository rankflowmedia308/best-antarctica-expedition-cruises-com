Editorial Policy & Ranking Methodology

How we evaluate and rank Antarctica expedition cruise operators — criteria, weights, and update cycle.

Ranking Criteria & Weights

Each operator is scored against six criteria. Scores are weighted and aggregated to produce the final ranking. Criteria were selected because they correlate directly with the quality of the expedition experience in Antarctica — not with marketing spend or brand visibility.

Criterion Weight What We Measure
Shore Time & Ship Size 25% Passenger capacity relative to IAATO 100-pax rule; simultaneous vs. rotation landing mechanics; average reported off-ship activity time per day
IAATO Compliance & Safety 20% Active IAATO membership; compliance record; bio-security protocol adherence; incident history; hull ice rating
Expedition Team Quality 20% Guide-to-guest ratio; specialist credentials (ornithology, glaciology, marine biology); years of polar expertise; open bridge policy
Activity Range 15% Optional add-ons available (kayaking, camping, helicopters, diving, ROV); standard vs. optional distinction; availability per voyage
Value for Money 10% Price-per-day relative to shore access, activities, and cabin quality within the operator's price tier; transparency of pricing
Traveler Feedback 10% Aggregated verified traveler reviews from multiple independent platforms; repeat booking rates where available

Independence Declaration

No operator pays for placement, review, or any form of coverage. No operator is consulted about their ranking prior to publication. Where affiliate relationships exist, they are disclosed and do not influence scoring or ranking position. Our editorial team does not accept complimentary voyages in exchange for coverage.

Update Cycle

Full ranking reviews are conducted annually, timed to coincide with the booking season (September–October). Rolling updates are made when significant changes occur: new vessel launches, fleet retirements, major pricing restructures, or changes in IAATO membership status. The date of last review is displayed on the main ranking page.
