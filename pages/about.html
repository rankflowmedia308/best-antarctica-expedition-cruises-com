About This Guide

Independent editorial coverage of Antarctica expedition cruising since 2021.

Our Editorial Team

This guide is produced by a team of three polar travel specialists with a combined 15+ years of direct Antarctica expedition experience. Our contributors have sailed the Drake Passage more than a dozen times, kayaked in Antarctic waters, and evaluated expedition programmes from Ushuaia to the Polar Circle. We do not identify ourselves publicly to preserve editorial independence from commercial relationships with operators and travel agencies.

How We Evaluate Operators

We evaluate each operator across six criteria weighted by their impact on the expedition experience. The full criteria and weights are published in our Editorial Policy. In summary:

  • 25% Shore time and ship size — simultaneous landing capability under the IAATO 100-passenger rule
  • 20% IAATO compliance and safety record
  • 20% Expedition team quality and guide-to-guest ratio
  • 15% Activity range (kayaking, camping, helicopters, ROV)
  • 10% Value for money relative to price tier
  • 10% Verified traveler feedback

Independence Statement

No Antarctica expedition cruise operator has paid for placement, review, or any form of coverage on this site. Rankings reflect our independent editorial assessment only. Where affiliate links are present, they do not influence ranking position or editorial content. The site was founded to address a gap in objective, operator-neutral guidance for the high-engagement research phase before Antarctica expedition bookings.
